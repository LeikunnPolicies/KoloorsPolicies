Koloors Privacy Policy

What is Koloors?

Koloors is a free to play game based around a simple point and shoot mechanism. Koloors is a property of Fabrizio Guidotti.

Privacy Policy

As the author we reserve the right to change this policy at any time if needed.

Access to device's functionalities.

Koloors does not require acess to any of the device functionalities to work, other than the internet acess to provide ads via AdMob.

Sharing on social media.

Koloors does not provide any way of sharing information on social media in any way nor encourages doing so.

Agreements

By using this app you are agreeing to the terms and conditions stated here, including subsequent changes and additions.

What kind of data do we collect?

Koloors does not directly save any of the user personal data anywhere on the internet. Koloors does save information about the user's advancements, achievement and purchased objects only on the user's device for gameplay purposes. Koloors uses AdMob to provide the ads to the end user.

AdMob by Google

AdMob is provided by Google Inc.

AdMob can collect user's data and can be disabled in the ad settings: https://adssettings.google.com/authenticated?hl=en_US. More information on the functioning of AdMob is available here: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites?hl=en-US.
